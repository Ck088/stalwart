ENTERPRISE RESOURCE PLANNING FOR PRE- ENGINEERED BUILDINGS

Pre-engineered buildings are the state-of-the-art steel solution to develop an efficient and cost-effective infrastructure.

PEBs offer ultimate design flexibility and an extremely short construction time (right from initial design to completion). They are supplied as a fully finished product along with steel structure, building accessories and roof cladding. They require no on-site fabrication or welding they can simply be bolted together as per specifications. PEBs are best suited for warehouses, sports halls, factories, workshops, distribution centers, cold storage, supermarkets, aircraft hangars or any ground t-storey construction.

ERP - PEB is a cutting edge technology software developed by Stalwart Software (P) Ltd. to serve the purpose of streamlining business process of customers who are into construction of steel structures.

It can seamlessly integrate Customer's with Marketing executive's and the Design/Detailing team with Production/Delivery Team.

Right from customer enquiry form, all the information related to the steel structure can be entered in an electronic format which inturn can be automatically forwarded to the design team. Design team can easily prepare steel structure design templates and deliver the customer directly through marketing executive. Once the design is approved by the customer, detailing team can start off with shop drawings very easily and forward the same to the production team.

Production of the steel structure can be planned based on project priority and material availability. Once the production schedule is finalized, we can simply pick & pack the material required for delivery at the customer's construction site. Based on the construction progress, the project manager can monitor and track the project status and progress.

A very huge and robust application with multi layer security and Role based access control can streamline construction and delivery process for the customers within timelines.
